The Story to Tell

  • Opp rides a steady base & is rebounding — overall weekly actives hold in the ~1,710–1,833 band while Opp actives range 527–698/wk (avg ~635), rebounding to 684 in the latest week (Jul 13) after a Jun 29 dip to 542.
  • Opp now reaches 68% of every active org — 1,614 of the 2,391 orgs that use the platform have used Opp; that's no longer a niche feature.
  • Penetration of the active base holds ~30–38% — weekly Opp reach of platform-active orgs stayed in that band across the sprint, ending at its high of 38.3% (Jul 13).
  • Depth is the gap — 58% of Opp-active orgs (939) used it <20 times over the window. Broad trial, not yet weekly habit.
  • Volume is top-heavy — top 10 orgs = 20%; Prutech alone = 11% of all Opp actions.
  • Next play: drive frequency in the 939 light-touch orgs & convert the 810 with zero usage — awareness is no longer the blocker.
