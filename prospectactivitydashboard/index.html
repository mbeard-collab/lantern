Methodology

Scope. All Prospect__c records created Jan 1 – Jun 18 2026 (3,337 rows; includes a few internal test records). Month tabs filter every panel. Sortable by any column; search and stage filters apply live.

Stage hours. "Curr hrs" = hours in the current stage (Salesforce Stage_Age_Hours, verified against stage-change history). Full per-stage breakdown is in the live Cowork dashboard.

Calls & emails. Salesforce Task records on the prospect's contact — calls (subtype Call) with duration from CallDurationInSeconds; emails (subtype Email). Voicemails/no-answers log 0 minutes.

Meetings. Salesforce Event records on the contact, de-duplicated by subject. "Mtgs" counts those already conducted; a +N badge marks upcoming/scheduled.

Rep coaching. Per rep: pipeline = sum of vendor-sales totals; high-value (HV) = accounts with vendor sales ≥$100M; HV covered = % of HV accounts with ≥1 call or meeting; outreach rate = (calls + meetings) / prospects, benchmarked against the team median; avg stage hrs = mean hours open prospects have sat in their current stage. Activity gaps = open prospects worth ≥$10M with zero calls and meetings; stalled = open, untouched, >1 week in current stage. Test/internal records are excluded from coaching.
